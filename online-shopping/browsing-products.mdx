The Joong Boo Market online store carries thousands of Korean and Asian grocery products. You can find what you're looking for by searching by name, browsing product categories, or filtering by department or diet type.

## Using the search bar

The fastest way to find a specific product is to use the search bar at the top of the page.

1. Click the search bar at the top of any page
2. Type a product name, brand, or keyword (e.g., "gochujang", "doenjang", "instant noodles")
3. Press **Enter** or click the search icon
4. Browse the results and click a product to view details

<Tip>
  Try searching in both English and Korean — many products are indexed by both their English and Korean names.
</Tip>

## Browsing by category

You can explore the full catalog by browsing product categories:

- **Produce** — Fresh vegetables, fruits, and herbs
- **Meat & Poultry** — Beef, pork, chicken, and more
- **Seafood** — Fish, shellfish, and dried seafood
- **Frozen Foods** — Dumplings, seafood, vegetables, and prepared meals
- **Pantry** — Rice, noodles, sauces, oils, and seasonings
- **Snacks & Beverages** — Korean snacks, teas, juices, and drinks
- **Dairy & Eggs** — Milk, yogurt, tofu, and eggs
- **Banchan & Prepared Foods** — Ready-to-eat Korean side dishes and hot food
- **Beauty & Household** — Korean skincare, cleaning supplies, and home essentials

Click any category in the navigation menu to browse all products in that department.

## Filtering and sorting results

When viewing a category or search results, you can refine your selection using filters:

- **Brand** — Filter by a specific brand name
- **Price range** — Set a minimum and maximum price
- **On sale** — Show only items with current promotions or discounts
- **In stock** — Filter to items available for your selected store location

You can also sort results by:
- Relevance (default)
- Price: low to high
- Price: high to low
- Newest arrivals

## Viewing product details

Click any product to see its full detail page, which includes:

- Product name, brand, and description
- Net weight or volume
- Price (and sale price, if applicable)
- Ingredients and nutritional information (where available)
- Customer reviews and ratings
- Related products you may also like

## Checking availability

Product availability can vary by store location. When you're logged in and have selected a store, the product page shows whether the item is in stock at your chosen location.

<Note>
  Online inventory is updated regularly, but availability can change quickly for popular or seasonal items. If an item you ordered is out of stock when your order is being prepared, you will be notified by email.
</Note>
