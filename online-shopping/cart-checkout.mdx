Once you've found the products you want, adding them to your cart and completing your purchase is quick and straightforward. You can apply digital coupons, use loyalty points, and choose between in-store pickup and home delivery.

## Adding items to your cart

On any product page or in search results:

1. Select the desired quantity using the **+** and **–** buttons
2. Click **Add to Cart**
3. A cart icon in the top navigation updates to show your item count

To view your cart at any time, click the cart icon in the top navigation bar.

## Managing your cart

In your cart, you can:

- **Adjust quantities** — Use the + and – buttons next to each item
- **Remove items** — Click the trash icon or set quantity to 0
- **Save for later** — Move items to a saved list if you're not ready to purchase
- **View product subtotals** — See the price per item and running total

## Choosing your fulfillment method

Before or during checkout, select how you want to receive your order:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="In-store pickup">
    1. Select **Pickup** as your fulfillment method
    2. Choose your preferred Joong Boo Market location (Chicago or Niles)
    3. Select an available pickup time window
    4. Complete your order — you'll receive an email when your order is ready

    See the [pickup guide](/orders/pickup) for more details.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Home delivery">
    1. Select **Delivery** as your fulfillment method
    2. Enter or select your delivery address
    3. Choose an available delivery time window
    4. Review delivery fees — they vary based on your address and order total

    See the [delivery guide](/orders/delivery) for more details.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Applying coupons and discounts

At checkout, you can apply digital coupons and loyalty points to reduce your total:

- **Digital coupons** — Coupons you've clipped from the [Deals page](/deals/coupons) are applied automatically at checkout if your cart meets the requirements
- **Promo codes** — Enter a promo code in the designated field at checkout
- **Loyalty points** — Choose to redeem available points for a discount on your order. See the [loyalty program guide](/deals/loyalty-program)

<Note>
  Only one promo code can be applied per order. Digital coupons can be combined with promo codes and loyalty point redemptions.
</Note>

## Completing payment

Joong Boo Market accepts the following payment methods online:

- Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover)
- Debit cards with a Visa or Mastercard logo
- Saved payment methods from your account

Enter your payment details or select a saved card, then click **Place Order** to complete your purchase. You will receive an order confirmation email immediately after your order is placed.

## After you place your order

Once your order is confirmed:

- You'll receive an **order confirmation email** with your order number and summary
- Your order status is visible in [Order History](/account/order-history)
- You'll receive a **ready notification** when your order is prepared (for pickup) or when your delivery is on its way

<Tip>
  Keep your order confirmation email — it contains your order number, which you'll need for pickup and for any customer service inquiries.
</Tip>
