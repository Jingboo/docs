A free Joong Boo Market account lets you place online orders, track your purchases, earn loyalty points, and access digital coupons. Creating an account takes less than two minutes.

## How to create an account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the Joong Boo Market website">
    Visit [joongboomarket.com](https://www.joongboomarket.com) and click **Sign Up** or **Create Account** in the top navigation bar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your information">
    Fill in the registration form with:
    - First and last name
    - Email address
    - Password (at least 8 characters)
    - Phone number (optional, used for order notifications)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify your email">
    Check your inbox for a verification email from Joong Boo Market. Click the confirmation link to activate your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start shopping">
    Once verified, you can log in and begin adding products to your cart. Your loyalty points account is activated automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What your account includes

Once you create an account, you get access to:

- **Online ordering** — Place orders for pickup or delivery
- **Order history** — View past orders and quickly reorder items
- **Loyalty rewards** — Earn points automatically on every purchase
- **Digital coupons** — Clip and apply coupons to your cart
- **Saved addresses** — Store delivery addresses for faster checkout
- **Saved payment methods** — Securely save cards for one-click checkout

## Signing in to your account

To sign in, click **Log In** on the Joong Boo Market website and enter your email and password. If you've forgotten your password:

1. Click **Forgot Password** on the login page
2. Enter the email address associated with your account
3. Check your email for a password reset link
4. Follow the link to create a new password

<Note>
  For security, password reset links expire after 30 minutes. If your link has expired, request a new one from the login page.
</Note>

## Updating your account information

After signing in, go to **My Account** to update your name, email, phone number, or password at any time. See the [account management guide](/account/manage-account) for more details.
