## Why shop online?

Shopping at Joong Boo Market online gives you:

- **Convenience** — Browse thousands of products from home and skip the lines
- **Weekly deals** — See all current specials and clip digital coupons before you shop
- **Order history** — Easily reorder your favorite products from past purchases
- **Loyalty points** — Earn rewards on every online order, just like shopping in-store
- **Flexible fulfillment** — Pick up in-store at your convenience or get delivery

## What's available online

Our online catalog includes products from every department:

- Fresh produce, Korean vegetables, and tropical fruits
- Fresh meats and seafood (selected cuts and varieties)
- Pantry staples: rice, noodles, sauces, seasonings, and canned goods
- Frozen foods and refrigerated items
- Snacks, beverages, and confectionery
- Korean beauty and household products

<Note>
  Some fresh items — such as live seafood and made-to-order banchan — are only available for in-store purchase. For the freshest selection of these items, visit us in person.
</Note>
