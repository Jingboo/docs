Every week, Joong Boo Market features special prices on a rotating selection of Korean and Asian groceries — from fresh produce and meats to pantry staples and snacks. Weekly specials are a great way to stock up on favorites at a lower price.

## How weekly specials work

- **New deals every week** — Specials rotate on a weekly basis, typically starting on Wednesday or Thursday
- **While supplies last** — Sale prices are available while promotional stock lasts
- **In-store and online** — Weekly specials apply to both in-store shopping and online orders
- **No clipping required** — Sale prices are automatically applied at checkout

## What's typically on sale

Weekly specials rotate across all departments, but commonly include:

- **Produce** — Seasonal Korean vegetables (bok choy, napa cabbage, Korean radish), citrus, and tropical fruits
- **Meat & seafood** — Galbi (short ribs), bulgogi-cut beef, pork belly, and fresh fish
- **Pantry** — Rice (20 lb+ bags), soy sauce, gochujang, doenjang, sesame oil, and ramen
- **Frozen foods** — Dumplings (mandu), frozen seafood, and prepared frozen meals
- **Snacks & beverages** — Korean snack packs, instant noodles, teas, and juices

<Tip>
  Stock up on pantry staples like rice, sauces, and noodles when they're on sale — these items store well and can save you significantly over time.
</Tip>
