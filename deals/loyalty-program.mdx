The Joong Boo Market loyalty rewards program lets you earn points on every purchase — in-store and online — and redeem them for discounts on future orders. Joining is free and takes less than a minute.

## How to join

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a free account">
    [Sign up for a Joong Boo Market account](/online-shopping/create-account) if you don't already have one. Your loyalty rewards account is created automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start earning points">
    Points are earned on every qualifying purchase, both in-store and online. In-store, make sure to provide your account phone number or scan your loyalty card at checkout.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Redeem your points">
    Once you've accumulated enough points, apply them at checkout to receive a discount on your order.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Earning points

You earn points on qualifying purchases at Joong Boo Market:

| Purchase type | Points earned |
|--------------|--------------|
| Regular-priced items | 1 point per $1 spent |
| Sale items | 1 point per $1 spent |
| Weekly special bonus items | 2x points (when indicated) |
| Online orders | Same as in-store |

<Note>
  Points are not earned on alcohol, tobacco, gift cards, lottery tickets, or delivery fees. Taxes are excluded from point calculations.
</Note>

## Redeeming points

When you have enough points accumulated, you can redeem them for a discount at checkout:

- **Online** — When checking out, select how many points you'd like to redeem in the **Loyalty Points** section
- **In-store** — Tell the cashier you'd like to redeem points before they process your transaction

A minimum number of points is required to redeem. The current redemption rate and minimum are displayed in your account under **My Rewards**.

## Checking your points balance

You can check your current points balance at any time:

1. Sign in to your account at [joongboomarket.com](https://www.joongboomarket.com)
2. Click your name or account icon
3. Select **My Rewards** or **Loyalty Points**

Your balance is also shown at checkout when you're signed in.

## Points expiration

Loyalty points expire if your account has no qualifying purchase activity for **12 consecutive months**. Keep your account active by making at least one purchase per year to maintain your points balance.

<Tip>
  Check your points balance before placing a large order — you may have enough points to redeem for a meaningful discount!
</Tip>

## Bonus point events

Periodically, Joong Boo Market runs bonus point promotions where you earn extra points on specific products or categories. These events are announced in the [weekly specials](/deals/weekly-specials) and by email newsletter. Sign up at [joongboomarket.com](https://www.joongboomarket.com) to receive promotions in your inbox.
