Joong Boo Market offers digital coupons that you can clip from our website and apply automatically at checkout. Coupons are a quick way to save on everyday products and weekly specials — no paper cutting required.

## How digital coupons work

Digital coupons are savings offers tied directly to your account. Once you clip a coupon, it's saved to your account and automatically applied when the qualifying product is in your cart.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Browse available coupons">
    Go to the **Deals** or **Coupons** section on [joongboomarket.com](https://www.joongboomarket.com) to see all currently available digital coupons.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clip coupons you want">
    Click **Clip** on any coupon to save it to your account. You can clip multiple coupons at once.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add qualifying products to your cart">
    Shop as usual. When a qualifying product is in your cart, the coupon discount is automatically applied.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your savings at checkout">
    Review your cart at checkout to see which coupons have been applied and your total savings.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Types of coupons

Joong Boo Market offers several types of savings:

| Coupon type | How it works |
|------------|-------------|
| **Product coupon** | Discount on a specific product or brand |
| **Category coupon** | Discount on any item from a product category (e.g., all ramen) |
| **Spend-and-save** | Discount when you spend a minimum amount (e.g., $5 off $50+) |
| **Weekly special coupon** | Extra discount stacked on top of the weekly sale price |

## Coupon terms and conditions

Each coupon has its own terms, including:

- **Expiration date** — Coupons expire on a set date. Expired coupons are automatically removed from your account.
- **Quantity limit** — Most coupons can only be used once per transaction
- **Product requirements** — The coupon applies only to the specified brand, size, or variety

Check the coupon details before clipping to understand the terms.

## Using promo codes

If you have a promo code (from a promotion, referral, or gift), you can apply it at checkout:

1. Add items to your cart and proceed to checkout
2. Find the **Promo Code** or **Discount Code** field
3. Enter your code and click **Apply**
4. The discount will be reflected in your order total

<Note>
  Only one promo code can be applied per order. Promo codes can generally be combined with digital coupons unless stated otherwise.
</Note>

## In-store coupons

Digital coupons clipped to your account also apply to in-store purchases. At the register, provide your account phone number or scan your loyalty card, and your clipped coupons will be applied automatically to qualifying items in your basket.

<Tip>
  Browse and clip coupons before your weekly shopping trip to maximize savings — many coupons are available for a limited time and expire on Wednesdays when the new weekly ad launches.
</Tip>
