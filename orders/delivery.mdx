Joong Boo Market offers home delivery so you can enjoy your favorite Korean and Asian groceries without leaving the house. Simply place your order online, select a delivery window, and we'll bring your groceries to your door.

## Delivery areas

Joong Boo Market delivers to addresses within the greater Chicago area, including surrounding suburbs. When entering your delivery address at checkout, the system will confirm whether delivery is available at your location.

<Note>
  Delivery availability may vary by date and time slot. If delivery is not available at your address, in-store pickup is always an option.
</Note>

## How to place a delivery order

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add items to your cart">
    Browse and add products to your cart as usual. See the [browsing guide](/online-shopping/browsing-products) if you need help.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select delivery at checkout">
    At checkout, choose **Delivery** as your fulfillment method.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your delivery address">
    Enter your home or business address. If you've saved addresses to your account, you can select one quickly. Confirm your address is within the delivery area.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a delivery window">
    Select an available date and time window for delivery. Windows are typically 2–3 hours wide.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and place your order">
    Review your order total, including delivery fee, and complete payment.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Delivery fees

Delivery fees are calculated at checkout based on your delivery address and order total. You'll see the exact fee before you confirm your order.

- Fees vary by distance and time slot
- Minimum order requirements may apply for delivery
- Delivery fees are non-refundable once an order has been dispatched

<Tip>
  Look for free delivery promotions in our [weekly specials](/deals/weekly-specials) — we occasionally offer discounted or free delivery on qualifying orders.
</Tip>

## Receiving your delivery

- Make sure someone is available at the delivery address during your selected window
- You'll receive a notification when your driver is on the way
- For perishable items, please bring your groceries inside promptly after delivery

## Delivery substitutions

If an item in your order is out of stock, our team will:

1. Attempt to substitute it with a comparable product
2. Notify you of the substitution by email before or at delivery
3. Give you the option to accept or reject the substitution

Rejected substitutions will be refunded to your original payment method.

## Minimum order for delivery

A minimum order amount is required for home delivery. The exact minimum is shown at checkout when you select delivery. If your cart is below the minimum, you can add more items or choose in-store pickup instead.
