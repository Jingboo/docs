In-store pickup lets you order groceries online and collect them at your nearest Joong Boo Market location — no waiting in lines or searching the aisles. Your order will be ready within your selected time window.

## How pickup works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Place your online order">
    Add products to your cart and select **Pickup** as your fulfillment method at checkout. Choose your preferred store location (Chicago - Kedzie or Niles - Golf Road).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a pickup window">
    Select an available date and time window for pickup. Time slots are generally available the same day or next day, depending on order size and store availability.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive your ready notification">
    When your order has been picked and packed, you'll receive an email and/or SMS notification letting you know it's ready for pickup.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Head to the store">
    Go to the designated pickup area in the store. Bring your order confirmation email or have your order number ready.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Collect your order">
    Show your order confirmation or provide your order number to a team member. They will bring out your order.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Where to pick up

When you arrive at the store, look for the **Online Order Pickup** sign or counter near the customer service area. Staff will assist you in retrieving your order.

- **Chicago - Kedzie Ave:** Pickup counter is located near the main entrance
- **Niles - Golf Road:** Pickup counter is located near customer service

<Tip>
  If you're unsure where to go when you arrive, ask any team member and they'll direct you to the pickup area.
</Tip>

## What to bring

When you arrive to pick up your order, have one of the following ready:

- Your **order confirmation email** (printed or shown on your phone)
- Your **order number** (found in your confirmation email or in [Order History](/account/order-history))

## Changing your pickup time

If you need to reschedule your pickup:

1. Go to [Order History](/account/order-history) in your account
2. Find your order and click **Manage Order**
3. Select a new available pickup time

<Note>
  Pickup time changes must be made at least 1 hour before your original scheduled pickup window. If you need to make a last-minute change, contact the store directly.
</Note>

## If you can't make it

If you're unable to pick up your order during your scheduled window:

- Contact the store by phone as soon as possible (see [Store Locations](/store-locations) for phone numbers)
- Orders that are not picked up within 24 hours of the scheduled window may be restocked and refunded

## Pickup fees

In-store pickup is **free** — there are no additional fees for choosing pickup over in-store shopping.
