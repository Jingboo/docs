After placing an order at Joong Boo Market online, you can track its progress from confirmation through to pickup or delivery. Status updates are available in your account and sent to you by email.

## Order status stages

Your order goes through the following stages:

| Status | What it means |
|--------|--------------|
| **Order Received** | Your order has been placed and confirmed |
| **Being Prepared** | Our team is picking and packing your items |
| **Ready for Pickup** | Your order is packed and waiting at the pickup counter |
| **Out for Delivery** | Your order is on its way to your address |
| **Completed** | Your order has been picked up or delivered |
| **Cancelled** | Your order was cancelled (see [returns and refunds](/orders/returns)) |

## How to check your order status

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in to your account">
    Go to [joongboomarket.com](https://www.joongboomarket.com) and sign in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Order History">
    Click your name or account icon, then select **Order History** or go to [account/order-history](/account/order-history).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find your order">
    Select the order you want to track. You'll see its current status, the list of items, and your selected pickup or delivery window.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Email and SMS notifications

You'll automatically receive status updates at key stages:

- **Order confirmation** — Immediately after placing your order
- **Order ready** — When your order is packed and ready for pickup, or when your delivery is dispatched
- **Delivery on the way** — When your driver is en route (delivery orders)
- **Order complete** — After pickup or delivery is confirmed

<Tip>
  Check your spam or junk folder if you don't see order notification emails. Add orders@joongboomarket.com to your contacts to ensure notifications reach your inbox.
</Tip>

## If your order is delayed

Occasionally, orders may take longer than expected due to high demand or item availability. If your order is significantly delayed beyond your selected window, you will be notified by email.

For urgent questions about your order status, contact the store directly. See [Store Locations](/store-locations) for phone numbers.
