Joong Boo Market wants you to be happy with every purchase. If something isn't right with your online order — a missing item, a damaged product, or an unwanted substitution — we'll make it right.

## Reporting an issue with your order

If your order has a problem, contact us as soon as possible after pickup or delivery:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in to your account">
    Go to [joongboomarket.com](https://www.joongboomarket.com) and sign in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Order History">
    Navigate to [Order History](/account/order-history) and select the affected order.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Report the issue">
    Click **Report a Problem** and select the issue type: missing item, wrong item, damaged item, or quality issue.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit your request">
    Describe the issue and submit. Our customer service team will review and respond within 1–2 business days.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Alternatively, you can contact us directly through the [Support page](/support/contact).

## Refund policy

| Issue type | Resolution |
|-----------|-----------|
| Missing item | Full refund for the missing item |
| Wrong item received | Refund or replacement at your preference |
| Damaged or spoiled item | Full refund for the affected item |
| Quality not as expected | Reviewed case-by-case; contact us |
| Unwanted substitution | Full refund for the substituted item |

Refunds are issued to your original payment method within **3–7 business days**, depending on your bank.

## Substitutions

When an item in your order is out of stock, our team may substitute it with a similar product of equal or greater value at no extra charge.

- You'll be notified of substitutions by email before or at delivery/pickup
- You can **reject** a substitution within 24 hours of receiving it
- Rejected substitutions are fully refunded

<Note>
  If you have strong preferences about substitutions (for example, you only want a specific brand), add a note in the **Order Notes** field at checkout or contact us directly.
</Note>

## Cancelling an order

You can cancel an order before it enters the **Being Prepared** stage:

1. Go to [Order History](/account/order-history) and find your order
2. Click **Cancel Order** if the option is available
3. A full refund will be issued to your original payment method within 3–7 business days

Once your order is being prepared or has been dispatched for delivery, it can no longer be cancelled online. In this case, contact the store directly as soon as possible.

## In-store returns

For items purchased in-store (not online), please bring the item and your receipt to the customer service counter at any Joong Boo Market location. Our team will assist you with an exchange or refund based on the condition of the item.
