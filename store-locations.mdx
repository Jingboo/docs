Joong Boo Market operates multiple locations across the greater Chicago area. Use the information below to find the store closest to you, get directions, or call ahead.

## Chicago — Kedzie Avenue (Main Store)

**Address:** 3333 N. Kedzie Ave, Chicago, IL 60618  
**Phone:** (773) 478-8801  
**Neighborhood:** Avondale / Logan Square

This is Joong Boo Market's flagship location, offering the widest selection of Korean and Asian groceries, fresh seafood, a full-service butcher counter, and a prepared foods section with ready-to-eat Korean dishes.

**Parking:** Free parking lot available on-site.

<Note>
  The Kedzie Avenue location features a large produce section and an in-store banchan (Korean side dishes) counter with freshly made selections daily.
</Note>

## Niles — Golf Road
