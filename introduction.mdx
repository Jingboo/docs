## What we offer

Joong Boo Market carries thousands of Korean and Asian products, including:

- **Fresh produce** — Korean vegetables, tropical fruits, and everyday staples
- **Meats & seafood** — Freshly cut beef, pork, and a wide variety of fish and shellfish
- **Banchan & prepared foods** — Ready-to-eat Korean side dishes and hot food
- **Pantry staples** — Sauces, noodles, rice, seasonings, and canned goods
- **Snacks & beverages** — Korean snacks, teas, juices, and soft drinks
- **Beauty & household** — Korean skincare, cleaning products, and household essentials

## How to use this guide

Browse the sections in the sidebar to learn how to:

- **Shop online** — Create an account, browse products, and place your first order
- **Save more** — Clip digital coupons and earn loyalty rewards
- **Manage orders** — Track your order, choose pickup or delivery, and handle returns
- **Get help** — Visit our [FAQ](/faq/general) or [contact our team](/support/contact)
