Joong Boo Market allows you to save payment methods to your account for convenient one-click checkout. You can add multiple cards, set a default, and remove cards you no longer use.

## Accepted payment methods

Joong Boo Market online accepts:

- **Credit cards** — Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover
- **Debit cards** — Visa and Mastercard debit cards with a billing address
- **Saved cards** — Previously saved payment methods in your account

<Note>
  Cash, EBT/SNAP, and store credit are accepted for in-store purchases but are not currently supported for online orders.
</Note>

## Adding a payment method

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Account Settings">
    Sign in and click your account icon. Select **Account Settings** → **Payment Methods**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Add Payment Method">
    Click **Add Card** or **+ New Payment Method**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter card details">
    Enter your card number, expiration date, CVV, and billing address, then click **Save Card**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Your card is saved securely and will be available at checkout.

## Setting a default payment method

If you have multiple saved cards, you can set one as your default for faster checkout:

1. Go to **Account Settings** → **Payment Methods**
2. Click **Set as Default** next to the card you want to use by default

Your default card is pre-selected at checkout, though you can always choose a different saved card or enter a new one.

## Removing a payment method

To remove a card from your account:

1. Go to **Account Settings** → **Payment Methods**
2. Click **Remove** or the trash icon next to the card
3. Confirm the removal

<Warning>
  If the card you're removing is the only payment method saved and you have a pending order, make sure to add a new card before removing the existing one.
</Warning>

## Payment security

Joong Boo Market uses industry-standard encryption to protect your payment information. Card numbers are tokenized and never stored in plain text. Our payment processing is handled by a certified PCI-DSS compliant payment provider.

<Tip>
  If your card is lost, stolen, or expires, update your payment method in account settings before placing your next order to avoid any delays.
</Tip>
