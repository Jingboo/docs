Your Joong Boo Market account gives you control over your personal information, notification preferences, and saved shopping details. You can update your account at any time from the account settings page.

## Accessing account settings

1. Go to [joongboomarket.com](https://www.joongboomarket.com) and sign in
2. Click your name or account icon in the top navigation
3. Select **Account Settings** or **My Profile**

## Updating personal information

In your account settings, you can update:

- **Name** — Your first and last name
- **Email address** — The email used for login and order notifications
- **Phone number** — Used for order SMS notifications and in-store loyalty card lookup
- **Password** — Change your password at any time

To update any of these:

1. Go to **Account Settings**
2. Click **Edit** next to the field you want to change
3. Enter the new information and click **Save**

<Note>
  If you change your email address, you may need to verify the new email before it takes effect. Check your inbox for a verification link.
</Note>

## Managing notification preferences

Control how Joong Boo Market communicates with you:

| Notification type | Channel options |
|------------------|----------------|
| Order confirmations | Email (always on) |
| Order ready / dispatched | Email and/or SMS |
| Weekly specials and promotions | Email (opt in/out) |
| Loyalty points updates | Email (opt in/out) |
| Coupon alerts | Email (opt in/out) |

To change your notification preferences:

1. Go to **Account Settings**
2. Click **Notification Preferences**
3. Toggle each notification type on or off
4. Click **Save Preferences**

## Managing saved addresses

Your saved delivery addresses make checkout faster. To manage them:

1. Go to **Account Settings** → **Saved Addresses**
2. Click **Add Address** to save a new delivery address
3. Click **Edit** or **Delete** next to any existing address

You can save multiple addresses and choose your default delivery address.

## Closing your account

If you'd like to close your Joong Boo Market account, contact our support team through the [Contact page](/support/contact). Note that closing your account will permanently remove your order history, loyalty points, and saved information.

<Warning>
  Account closure is permanent. Any unused loyalty points will be forfeited when your account is closed.
</Warning>
