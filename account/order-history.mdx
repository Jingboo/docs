Your Joong Boo Market order history keeps a record of all your online orders in one place. You can check the status of current orders, view receipts, and reorder products from past purchases with a single click.

## Accessing your order history

1. Sign in to [joongboomarket.com](https://www.joongboomarket.com)
2. Click your account icon in the top navigation
3. Select **Order History**

You'll see a list of your orders, from most recent to oldest, with the order date, total, fulfillment method (pickup or delivery), and current status.

## Reordering from a past order

To quickly reorder the same products:

1. Go to **Order History** and find the order you want to repeat
2. Click **Reorder** or **Add All to Cart**
3. Your cart will be populated with the same items — remove or adjust any as needed
4. Proceed to checkout to place your new order

<Note>
  Prices and availability may have changed since your original order. Review your cart before checking out to confirm current prices.
</Note>

## Viewing order receipts

For each completed order, you can view a detailed receipt:

1. Click on any order in your Order History
2. Select **View Receipt** or **Order Details**
3. You'll see a full breakdown including:
   - Items ordered and quantities
   - Regular prices and any discounts applied
   - Loyalty points earned
   - Delivery fee (if applicable)
   - Total charged

You can also print or save the receipt as a PDF from the receipt page.

## Order history availability

Your complete order history is stored in your account as long as your account is active. Orders are available for viewing indefinitely — so you can reference past purchases, track spending, and reorder favorites at any time.

<Tip>
  Use your order history as a shopping reference — it's a great way to remember the exact products and brands you've ordered before and loved.
</Tip>
