Have questions about specific products or finding what you need at Joong Boo Market? Browse the answers below or [contact us](/support/contact) if you need personalized assistance.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I find a specific product?">
    Use the search bar on the [Joong Boo Market website](https://www.joongboomarket.com) to search by product name, brand, or keyword. You can search in English or Korean. If you're shopping in-store, ask any team member for help locating a product.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do you carry products from specific brands?">
    Yes, we carry a wide range of well-known Korean and Asian brands including CJ, Nongshim, Ottogi, Sempio, Bibigo, Pulmuone, and many others. Use the brand filter in our online store to browse by brand.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Are the products fresh? How often is produce restocked?">
    Our produce section is restocked daily. Fresh meats and seafood are also received regularly. For the best selection of fresh items, we recommend shopping in the morning. If you have questions about a specific item's freshness, ask a team member in the relevant department.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do you carry organic or non-GMO products?">
    Yes, we carry a selection of organic produce and some non-GMO labeled products. Look for the organic label in the produce section or filter for organic items in our online store.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I place a special order for a large quantity of a specific product?">
    Yes. For large quantity orders — such as catering-sized portions of meat or bulk pantry items — call your nearest store directly to inquire about special orders. See [Store Locations](/store-locations) for phone numbers.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do you sell live seafood?">
    Yes, select live seafood is available at our store locations, particularly the Chicago Kedzie Avenue location. Live seafood availability varies by season and supply. We recommend calling ahead to confirm availability before visiting specifically for live seafood.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Are product ingredients listed online?">
    Ingredient information is available on individual product pages where the manufacturer provides it. If you need ingredient details for an item not listed online, contact us or check the product label in-store.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do you carry halal or kosher products?">
    We carry some halal and kosher certified products. Look for the relevant certification symbols on product labels. For specific halal or kosher product inquiries, contact the store or ask a team member during your visit.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
