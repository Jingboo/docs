## About Joong Boo Market

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Where are Joong Boo Market stores located?">
    Joong Boo Market has two locations in the Chicago area:

    - **Chicago — Kedzie Ave:** 3333 N. Kedzie Ave, Chicago, IL 60618 | (773) 478-8801
    - **Niles — Golf Road:** 8800 N. Milwaukee Ave, Niles, IL 60714 | (847) 298-0400

    See the full [Store Locations](/store-locations) page for directions and parking details.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What are the store hours?">
    Both Joong Boo Market locations are open daily from **8:00 AM to 9:00 PM**. Hours may change on major holidays — check the [Store Hours](/store-hours) page for the latest information.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What types of products does Joong Boo Market carry?">
    Joong Boo Market specializes in Korean and Asian groceries, including:
    - Fresh produce, Korean vegetables, and tropical fruits
    - Fresh meats, seafood, and a full-service butcher counter
    - Banchan (Korean side dishes) and prepared foods
    - Pantry staples: rice, noodles, sauces, seasonings
    - Snacks, beverages, and confectionery
    - Korean beauty and household products
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does Joong Boo Market carry products from specific Asian countries?">
    Yes. While Joong Boo Market specializes in Korean products, we also carry a wide selection of Japanese, Chinese, Vietnamese, Thai, Filipino, and other Asian grocery products. Our selection reflects the diverse tastes of the Chicago Asian community.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I find Korean-specific ingredients for traditional recipes?">
    Absolutely. We carry a comprehensive range of authentic Korean ingredients, including gochujang, doenjang, ganjang, sesame oil, rice wine, dried anchovies (myulchi), various kimchi varieties, tteok (rice cakes), and much more.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Online shopping

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do I need an account to shop online?">
    Yes, you need a free Joong Boo Market account to place online orders. [Create your account here](/online-shopping/create-account) — it only takes a minute and gives you access to order tracking, loyalty points, and digital coupons.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is online pricing the same as in-store pricing?">
    Yes, prices for most products are the same online and in-store. Weekly specials and sale prices apply to both online and in-store purchases.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I order fresh meat and seafood online?">
    Yes, selected cuts of fresh meat and seafood are available for online order. For specialty cuts, custom orders, or live seafood, we recommend visiting the store in person.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
