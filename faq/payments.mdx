Find answers to frequently asked questions about paying for your Joong Boo Market purchases — both online and in-store.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What payment methods are accepted online?">
    Joong Boo Market's online store accepts:
    - Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover credit cards
    - Visa and Mastercard debit cards
    - Saved payment methods in your account

    See [Payment Methods](/account/payment-methods) to manage your saved cards.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What payment methods are accepted in-store?">
    In-store, we accept:
    - Cash
    - Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover)
    - Debit cards
    - EBT/SNAP cards (for eligible food items)
    - Joong Boo Market gift cards

    Note that EBT/SNAP is not currently supported for online orders.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do you accept EBT/SNAP for online orders?">
    EBT/SNAP is accepted for eligible in-store purchases but is not currently supported for online orders. If you use EBT/SNAP, please shop in-store at either location.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do you sell or accept Joong Boo Market gift cards?">
    Yes, Joong Boo Market gift cards are available for purchase in-store and can be used for in-store purchases. Contact us or visit a store for more information about gift card availability.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="When will I be charged for my online order?">
    Your payment method is charged when your order is confirmed. If any items are unavailable and removed from your order, the refund for those items is issued within 3–7 business days.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do refunds work?">
    Refunds for online orders are issued to your original payment method. Processing time is typically 3–7 business days, depending on your bank or card issuer. See [Returns & Refunds](/orders/returns) for more details.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is it safe to save my card online?">
    Yes. Joong Boo Market uses industry-standard encryption and PCI-DSS compliant payment processing. Your card number is never stored in plain text — it is tokenized securely. See [Payment Methods](/account/payment-methods) for more.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I use multiple payment methods for one order?">
    Currently, online orders support one payment method per transaction. You can apply digital coupons and loyalty point redemptions in addition to your payment card to reduce your total.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
