Get answers to frequently asked questions about placing and managing orders at Joong Boo Market. For issues with a specific order, visit [Returns & Refunds](/orders/returns) or [Contact Support](/support/contact).

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I place an online order?">
    To place an order:
    1. [Create a free account](/online-shopping/create-account) or sign in
    2. Browse products and add them to your cart
    3. At checkout, choose pickup or delivery
    4. Enter payment and confirm your order

    See the full [Cart & Checkout guide](/online-shopping/cart-checkout) for step-by-step instructions.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I change my order after placing it?">
    Orders can be modified while they are still in **Order Received** status. Once preparation begins, changes are not possible online. Contact the store directly as soon as possible if you need to make a late change.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I cancel my order?">
    You can cancel your order from [Order History](/account/order-history) before it enters the **Being Prepared** stage. Click **Cancel Order** on the order detail page. Once preparation starts, cancellations must be handled by contacting the store directly.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens if an item I ordered is out of stock?">
    If an item is out of stock when your order is being prepared, our team will either:
    - Substitute it with a similar product of equal or greater value
    - Remove it from your order and issue a refund

    You'll be notified by email of any substitutions or removals. Substitutions can be rejected within 24 hours for a full refund. See [Returns & Refunds](/orders/returns) for more.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How long does it take for my order to be ready for pickup?">
    Same-day pickup is often available when orders are placed in the morning. The exact availability depends on store volume and the items in your order. You can select your preferred pickup time window at checkout, and you'll receive a notification when your order is ready.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is the minimum order for delivery?">
    A minimum order amount is required for home delivery. The exact minimum is shown at checkout when you select the delivery option. If your cart is below the minimum, you can add more items or switch to in-store pickup.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I reorder from a past purchase?">
    Yes. Go to [Order History](/account/order-history), find a past order, and click **Reorder** to add all items to your cart. Review your cart before checkout, as prices and availability may have changed.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I track my order?">
    After placing an order, go to [Order History](/account/order-history) to check the current status. You'll also receive email notifications at key stages. See the [Order Tracking](/orders/tracking) guide for more details.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
