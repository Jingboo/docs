## What we do with your feedback

Every piece of feedback is reviewed by our team. Customer feedback directly influences:

- **Product selection** — We use customer requests to evaluate adding new products or brands
- **Store improvements** — Suggestions about store layout, signage, or services help us make the store better
- **Online shopping** — Technical issues, missing features, and usability concerns are reviewed by our team
- **Customer service** — We take service feedback seriously and use it in team training

## Product requests

If there's a specific product, brand, or item you'd like to see at Joong Boo Market, let us know. While we can't guarantee every requested product will be stocked, our buyers review customer requests regularly when updating our product selection.

To request a product:
1. [Contact us](/support/contact) by phone or email
2. Share the product name, brand, and ideally the country of origin or where you've seen it sold
3. We'll look into availability and let you know if we can source it

## Report a store or service issue

If you experienced a problem during an in-store or online visit — including a customer service concern, a cleanliness issue, or a safety matter — please [contact us directly](/support/contact) and describe the issue in detail. We take all reports seriously and will follow up promptly.

<Tip>
  The most helpful feedback includes specific details: what happened, when, at which location, and what you expected. This helps us address issues quickly and accurately.
</Tip>
