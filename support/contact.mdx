Our team is here to help with any questions about your order, account, products, or store services. Reach us using the contact options below.

## Contact by phone

The fastest way to get help is to call your nearest Joong Boo Market location directly:

| Location | Phone | Hours |
|----------|-------|-------|
| Chicago — Kedzie Ave | (773) 478-8801 | 8:00 AM – 9:00 PM daily |
| Niles — Golf Road | (847) 298-0400 | 8:00 AM – 9:00 PM daily |

Our in-store staff can help with:
- Order status and pickup inquiries
- Product availability and special orders
- Store services and department-specific questions
- Accessibility accommodations

## Contact by email or online form

For non-urgent inquiries, you can reach us via email or the online contact form at [joongboomarket.com](https://www.joongboomarket.com). Common reasons to reach out by email include:

- Order issues or refund requests
- Account help
- Feedback or suggestions
- Wholesale or bulk order inquiries

<Note>
  Online inquiries are typically responded to within 1–2 business days. For urgent matters such as a time-sensitive order, please call the store directly.
</Note>

## Online order issues

If you have a problem with an online order (missing item, wrong item, damage), you can also report it directly through your account:

1. Go to [Order History](/account/order-history)
2. Select the affected order
3. Click **Report a Problem**

This is the fastest way to initiate a refund or replacement request. See [Returns & Refunds](/orders/returns) for details.

## Visit us in-store

Our team at both locations is happy to assist you in person with any questions. See the [Store Locations](/store-locations) page for addresses and directions.

<Tip>
  If you're visiting specifically to resolve an order issue, bring your order confirmation email or have your order number ready for faster service.
</Tip>
