## Holiday hours

During major US holidays, Joong Boo Market may operate on reduced hours or close early. We will update this page with any changes as holidays approach. You can also:

- Call your local store directly for the most up-to-date hours
- Check our [store locations page](/store-locations) for phone numbers
- Follow Joong Boo Market on social media for holiday hour announcements

## Online order pickup hours

If you placed an online order for in-store pickup, pickup windows are available during regular store hours. You will receive an email confirmation with your specific pickup time window when your order is ready.

<Tip>
  For the freshest selection — especially for produce and seafood — we recommend shopping in the morning, shortly after the store opens.
</Tip>
